US President Trump: Fed rates should be at 1%, second Putin meeting uncertain
NEWS | 08/13/2025 16:47:38 GMT | Chaitanya
United States (US) President Trump delivered his latest thoughts on his efforts to be the primary peace deal broker between Russia and Ukraine on Wednesday, and dipped back into the Reserve (Fed) pool of usual statements to his own estimations of where interest rates should be.

President Trump commented that there will be ( also might not be) a second meeting between himself and Russian President Vladimir Putin if he does (or doesn't) receive satisfactory concessions from Putin. Trump also shared his "paper calculation" t hat Fed interest rates should be at or near 23 and 36. Donald Trump's math on the matter was not provided.
